About Complete Cool Air

Air Conditioning Repair & HVAC Solutions
Complete Cool Air offers a full line of heating and air conditioning products and services, including repair and maintenance, service programs, system replacement, heating repair, heat pump service, and more.

We’re more than technicians—we’re your neighbors. For over 10 years we’ve helped families lower energy bills, stay comfortable, and enjoy peace of mind. We provide 24/7 emergency service, factory-trained pros, and a family company you can trust with:

  • Written warranty on all repairs
  • Lifetime workmanship guarantee
  • Rapid emergency response

Family-owned & operated by Paul Levi, a DFW veteran with 30+ years in HVAC and military service. Our mission: create healthy, quiet, perfectly-balanced indoor environments—clean air, ideal humidity, consistent temperatures, and whisper-quiet operation.

Since 2005, Complete Cool Air has delivered fast, honest HVAC service across DFW. Our licensed technicians handle repairs, installs, and tune-ups with a 100% satisfaction guarantee.
